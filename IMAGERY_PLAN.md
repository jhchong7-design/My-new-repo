# 시온산교회 - Imagery Enhancement Plan

## Pages Requiring Unique Imagery

### 1. **index.html** (Main Landing Page)
- Hero banner: Church exterior or worship gathering
- Welcome section: Pastoral team or community
- Ministry showcase: Various ministry activities

### 2. **gallery.html** (Photo Gallery)
- Category headers: Worship, Community, Mission, Events
- Gallery items: 12-15 unique church photos

### 3. **dashboard.html** (Member Dashboard)
- Profile section: Church interior or sanctuary
- Statistics banners: Community gathering images
- Event previews: Recent church events

### 4. **events.html** (Events Calendar)
- Calendar header: Church community gathering
- Event cards: Various church event photos

### 5. **sermons.html** (Video Page)
- Video thumbnail: Pastor preaching
- Sermon series: Worship atmosphere

### 6. **about.html** (About Page)
- Leadership section: Pastoral team
- History section: Church building
- Faith statement: Worship scene

1. **Church Exterior/Building**

3. **Worship Service (People praying, singing)**
4. **Pastor Preaching**
5. **Community Fellowship**
6. **Children/Youth Ministry**
7. **Mission Work**
8. **Special Events/Conferences**
