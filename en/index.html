About the Professor
운영자 소개

Jung Joong-Ho (정중호)

Professor Emeritus, Dept. of Christian Studies, Keimyung University · Pastor, Presbyterian Church of Korea

Professor Jung devoted his career to Old Testament research and education at Keimyung University. He graduated from Presbyterian Theological Seminary, studied at Columbia Theological Seminary, earned his Ph.D. from Emory University, and conducted research at Princeton Theological Seminary and the Graduate Theological Union (GTU) at Berkeley.

He has served as President of the Korean Bible Society, President of the Korean Old Testament Society, Director of the Word of God Research Institute, Editor-in-Chief of the Old Testament Forum, and Director of the Asian Christianity Research Institute. He has published more than 55 academic papers and numerous books.
