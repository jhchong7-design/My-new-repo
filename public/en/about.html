Professor Jung Joong-Ho (정중호)

Professor Emeritus · Department of Christian Studies · Keimyung University

Professor Jung Joong-Ho is Professor Emeritus of Old Testament Studies at Keimyung University's Department of Christian Studies, and an ordained pastor of the Presbyterian Church of Korea. He has devoted his entire academic career to Old Testament research and education, playing a pioneering role in illuminating the history of Korean Bible translation and interpretation.

His research has revealed the unique character of Korean Christian history — that Koreans obtained and read the Bible on their own, organized faith communities, and even performed baptisms before any missionaries arrived on Korean soil.

📖 Research Areas

Professor Jung's primary field is Old Testament Studies, with particular focus on the History of Israel, Old Testament Prophetic Literature, the history of Korean Bible translation and interpretation, Korean Thought and the Bible, the dialogue between Science and Scripture, and Asian Christianity research.
